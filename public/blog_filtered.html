Blog Posts by Dan Moulthrop

I'm Curator of Conversation here at the Civic Commons and one of the co-founders, as well. From 2005 to 2010, I was a program host for ideastream (90.3 WCPN and WVIZ-PBS). I am co-author (with Dave Eggers and Ninive Calegari) of "Teachers Have it Easy: The Big Sacrifices and Small Salaries of America's Teachers." (The New Press, 2005). Once upon a time, I taught high school English in California. My wife and I have three children, and we live in Shaker Heights, Ohio.

Wait, so who exactly are the job creators?

Monday, December 12, 2011, Topic: Transportation

By: Dan Moulthrop

Blame it on Guy Raz. I had planned on another look-back installment, but ever since I heard a piece he did Saturday night on All Things Considered, I haven't been able to stop thinking about the current debate over taxation--you know, the one that has been framed as class warfare and an impingement on job creation. This is one of those beautiful, George Lakoff moments, where the frame has become more important that the issue. Though what's truly great about what's happening right now is that the coverage seems to be catching up to the frame and totally undermining it.
